Other creative projects

These hobby projects don't show what I can do on a professional level but they are definitely part of what I want to represent as a person.

NoPixel crochet fanart

I have crochted a lot of fanart for nopixel streamers and character. Either their stream logo as a crocheted amigurumi or a character. To me fanart is a great way to express my apreciation to streamers in combination with self expression.

Wedding outfit

For my wedding I decided I wanted to created my entire outfit myself. I started with the idea of embroidered white vans, I designed my own dress with matching embrodery and to finish it all I incorperated the same embrodery in my jewelry.

Legend of Zelda - Link - Cosplay

For a rennasance fair I made this Link cosplay. It is based on the hylean armor from Breath of the Wild and Tears of the Kingdom. I made the tunic from an old shirt and machine embroidered it. I made fake leather straps and armor. I 3D printed and painted the sword. I 3D printed the sheathed and covered it with fake leather. Lastly I made the cape from an old coat and dry felted the pattern on it.
