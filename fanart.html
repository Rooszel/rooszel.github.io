Other creative projects

These hobby projects don't show what I can do on a professional level but they are definitely part of what I want to represent as a person.

NoPixel crochet fanart

2 years ago I set out as my goal to show people that crochet and other forms of fanart are still great ways to express apreciation to streamers as well as a form of self-expression. I want to inspire others to go out and make whatever form of art they want and feel comfortable with sharing.
Since then I've heard multiple people tell me I inspired them to make embroidery fanart, start up crocheting again or just look up to my work and made it their goal to be able to make their own self drafted amigurumi.
To make my amigurumi, I start by imagining the project in 3D. I sketch the rough size and plan which crochet stitches, colors, and techniques Iâ€™ll need. I also estimate how many stitches to add or remove in different places to create the right shape. After planning, I experiment through trial and error until I get the form I want. Once it works, I write everything down so I can reproduce the pattern.


Wedding outfit

For my wedding, I decided I wanted to created my entire outfit myself. I started with the idea of embroidered white Vans, I designed my own dress with matching embroidery and to finish it all I incorperated the same embroidery in my jewelry.


IrishGT shorts

For IrishGT I wanted to help spread some of his roleplay so I offered to make, edit, subtitle and upload shorts for him.


Legend of Zelda - Link - Cosplay

For a Renaissance fair I made this Link cosplay. It is based on the Hylian armor from Zelda:Breath of the Wild and Zelda:Tears of the Kingdom. I made the tunic from an old shirt and machine embroidered it. I made fake leather straps and armor. I 3D printed and painted the sword. I 3D printed the sheathed and covered it with fake leather. Lastly I made the cape from an old coat and dry felted the pattern on it.

Journalist for the World Scout Jamboree newspaper

During the 23rd World scout Jamboree I was part of the newspaper team. The World Scout Jamboree is the biggest gathering of scouts in the world. The circulation of the newspaper was 35.000 during the 10 days of the camp. I got to interview the grandson of the founder of the scouts movement and the United Nations Secretary General's envoy on Youth. I also worked a general newspaper team, from design decision to getting random stories. It was a great experience for my English as well as going out there and talking to a lot of different people.
